- Select a Tile to get more information about its objective


- Players can mark a Tile as "Reserved" or "Completed" by selecting a Tile & clicking the "Edit" button.

- Marking a Tile as "Reserved" indicates to your team that you would like to be the player responsible for the objective.

- Marking a Tile as "Completed" indicates to your team that you have successfully completed the objective & submitted a screenshot on Discord.


- Please see the "Rules" page for more detailed information regarding Tiles and progression.
