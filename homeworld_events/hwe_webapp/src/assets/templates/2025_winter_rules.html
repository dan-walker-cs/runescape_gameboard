General Gameplay

- RESTRICTION: No "DOLO" strats. 1 account per player may be used to participate.
- RESTRICTION: You may not group with players outside of the event for any content, however you may group with players from the opposing team.

- All members of a team start on the board's grey center tile.
- Players may work on any tile adjacent to the start tile or a completed tile.
- All members of a team share progress on the board & on a tile.

- i.e. If I complete a tile, unlocking those adjacent, any of my team members can begin the unlocked adjacent tile(s).

- i.e. If a tile requires 500 monkey laps, 5 team members could each do 100 laps to complete the objective.


Special Tiles

Warp Tiles

- These tiles are indicated by pulsing, colored borders.
- When completed, the team can move to any of the other same-colored tiles on the board.
- If the destination tile has an objective, it must be completed before unlocking adjacent tiles.
- If the destination tile has no objective, adjacent tiles are unlocked immediately.

Requirement Tiles

- These objectives are the central group of red tiles at the top of the board.
- Rather than pathing or warping, these are each unlocked by a different requirement, usually a related tile somewhere on the board.
- These tiles may be selected to see their pre-requisite(s).

Scoring

Standard

Easy tiles: 1 point(s)

- Pale green tiles

Medium tiles: 2 point(s)

- Yellow tiles

Hard tiles: 3 point(s)

- Red tiles

Bonus

- 5 bonus points are earned for each completed "island" of tiles.
