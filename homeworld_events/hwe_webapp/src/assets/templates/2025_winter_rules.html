General

- RESTRICTION: No "DOLO" strats. 1 account per player may be used to participate. - RESTRICTION: You may not group with players outside of the event for any content, however you may group with players from the opposing team. - All members of a team start on the board's grey center tile.
- Players may work on any tile adjacent to the start tile or a completed tile.
- All members of a team share progress on the board & on a tile.

- i.e. If I complete a tile, unlocking those adjacent, any of my team members can begin the unlocked adjacent tile(s).

- i.e. If a tile requires 500 monkey laps, 5 team members could each do 100 laps to complete the objective.
